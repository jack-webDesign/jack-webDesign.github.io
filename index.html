Expectations

Jack Sosler

Dart 230

Everyday is a new opportunity

Everyday, you always encounter the unexpected

There are things you can expect or forsee

But, the unexpected always affects the forseen

To be prepared for the unexpected, what can you prepare?

Can you put your faith into your expectations?

Adjusting through the unexpected

But only until the unexpected occurs

Does anything ever go as planned?

Expectations should not rely on uncertainty

Can you adapt everyday?

Until you can never have expections
